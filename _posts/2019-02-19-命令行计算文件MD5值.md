#### Windows命令行查看文件的MD5
```shell
certutil -hashfile <file> MD5
certutil -hashfile <file> SHA1
certutil -hashfile <file> SHA256
```
---

#### Linux命令行查看文件的MD5
```shell
md5sum <file>
```
